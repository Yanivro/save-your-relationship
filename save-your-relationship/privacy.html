Privacy

Last updated June 2026

This page explains what True Contact (Yaniv Rose) collects when you visit this site or buy Save Your Relationship, and what we do with it. The short version: we collect the minimum needed to deliver the product, send the emails you signed up for, and understand how the page performs. We don't sell your data.

What we collect

  • Your email address, when you buy the product or opt in on the site, so we can deliver it and send the 30-day email series.
  • Purchase details, handled by our checkout provider Gumroad (we never see your full card details).
  • Usage data, such as pages viewed and clicks, through analytics tools that use cookies and similar technology.

The tools we use

  • Gumroad — checkout and product delivery.
  • MailerLite — the email series and any newsletters.
  • Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity — to see how the page is used and where it can be clearer.
  • Meta Pixel — to measure and improve the ads that bring people here.

Each of these processes data under its own privacy policy.

How we use it

To deliver what you bought, send the emails you asked for, keep the site working, measure our advertising, and improve the writing and the page. That's it.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in any message. You can ask us to show you or delete the personal data we hold by emailing yaniv@thetruecontact.com. You can also block or clear cookies in your browser settings.

Contact

Any privacy question, write to yaniv@thetruecontact.com.
